SECTION 1: PARTIES AND DEFINITIONS

1.1 This Professional Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of March 15, 2024, between:

    SERVICE PROVIDER: NovaTech Solutions Pvt. Ltd., a company incorporated under the laws of India, having its registered office at 45 Tech Park, Bangalore, Karnataka 560001 ("Provider")

    CLIENT: Meridian Enterprises LLC, a limited liability company organized under the laws of Delaware, USA, having its principal office at 200 Commerce Street, Wilmington, DE 19801 ("Client")

1.2 Definitions:
    - "Services" refers to AI-powered data analytics and software development services as described in Schedule A.
    - "Deliverables" means all software, reports, models, and documentation produced under this Agreement.
    - "Confidential Information" means any non-public information disclosed by either party.

---

SECTION 2: SCOPE OF SERVICES

2.1 The Provider agrees to deliver the following services:
    (a) Development of a custom machine learning pipeline for customer churn prediction.
    (b) Integration of the pipeline with Client's existing CRM system (Salesforce).
    (c) Monthly performance reports for a period of 12 months post-deployment.
    (d) Staff training sessions (minimum 3 sessions) for Client's data team.

2.2 All services shall be performed remotely unless otherwise agreed in writing.

2.3 The Provider may engage qualified subcontractors with prior written approval from the Client. The Provider remains fully responsible for subcontractor performance.

---

SECTION 3: PROJECT TIMELINE AND MILESTONES

3.1 Project Commencement Date: April 1, 2024
3.2 Expected Completion Date: September 30, 2024

3.3 Key Milestones:
    - Milestone 1: Requirements gathering and data audit — April 30, 2024
    - Milestone 2: ML model prototype (v0.1) — June 15, 2024
    - Milestone 3: CRM integration complete — July 31, 2024
    - Milestone 4: User Acceptance Testing (UAT) — August 31, 2024
    - Milestone 5: Full deployment and handover — September 30, 2024

3.4 Delays caused by Client's failure to provide required data or approvals shall not count against Provider's timeline obligations.

SECTION 4: PAYMENT TERMS

4.1 Total Contract Value: USD 120,000

4.2 Payment Schedule:
    - 30% upfront upon signing: USD 36,000 (due April 1, 2024)
    - 30% upon Milestone 3 completion: USD 36,000 (due August 1, 2024)
    - 40% upon final delivery: USD 48,000 (due October 15, 2024)

4.3 All invoices are payable within 14 days of issuance.

4.4 Late payments will accrue interest at 1.5% per month on the outstanding balance.

4.5 The Client may not withhold payment citing minor bugs or cosmetic issues that do not affect core functionality.

SECTION 5: INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

5.1 All Deliverables created specifically for the Client under this Agreement shall become the exclusive property of the Client upon receipt of full payment.

5.2 The Provider retains ownership of all pre-existing tools, frameworks, and libraries ("Background IP") used in the development process.

5.3 The Provider is granted a non-exclusive, royalty-free license to showcase the project as a portfolio reference (excluding confidential data), unless the Client objects in writing within 30 days of project completion.

---

SECTION 6: CONFIDENTIALITY

6.1 Both parties agree to maintain strict confidentiality of all Confidential Information for a period of 3 years following the termination of this Agreement.

6.2 Confidential Information may be disclosed only to employees or contractors who need to know such information and are bound by confidentiality obligations.

6.3 The following are not considered Confidential Information:
    (a) Information already in the public domain.
    (b) Information independently developed without reference to Confidential Information.
    (c) Information required to be disclosed by law or court order.

---

SECTION 7: RISK AND LIABILITY

7.1 LIMITATION OF LIABILITY: In no event shall either party's total liability exceed the total fees paid under this Agreement in the 6 months preceding the claim.

7.2 Neither party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, special, or consequential damages, including loss of revenue or data.

7.3 RISK AREAS IDENTIFIED:
    (a) Data Quality Risk: The accuracy of the ML model is dependent on the quality of historical data provided by the Client. Provider does not warrant specific model accuracy percentages.
    (b) Integration Risk: Delays in CRM integration may occur due to Salesforce API limitations or third-party outages outside Provider's control.
    (c) Regulatory Risk: The Client is solely responsible for ensuring that the use of AI-generated predictions complies with applicable laws, including GDPR and CCPA.
    (d) Security Risk: The Client must ensure all data shared with Provider is anonymized or compliant with applicable data protection laws prior to transfer.

---

SECTION 8: TERMINATION

8.1 Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days written notice.

8.2 The Client may terminate immediately for cause if the Provider commits a material breach that remains uncured for 15 days after written notice.

8.3 Upon termination, the Client shall pay for all work completed up to the termination date on a pro-rata basis.

8.4 Sections 5, 6, and 7 shall survive termination of this Agreement.

---

SECTION 9: DISPUTE RESOLUTION

9.1 The parties shall first attempt to resolve any dispute through good-faith negotiation for a period of 30 days.

9.2 Unresolved disputes shall be submitted to binding arbitration under the rules of the International Chamber of Commerce (ICC), with arbitration seated in Singapore.

9.3 The governing law of this Agreement is the law of the State of Delaware, USA.

10.1 This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior discussions.
10.2 Amendments must be made in writing and signed by authorized representatives of both parties.
10.3 If any provision is found unenforceable, the remaining provisions remain in full force.
